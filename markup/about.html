About Luminescence

Stranded on a desert planet with nothing but their wits and some really fucking big light cannons, two rival factions battle for control of their (conveniently symmetrical) surroundings, and a chance to survive their plight. Each player controls a set of individual units.

Each player must program his units to coordinate with each other and destroy the enemy. The player who destroys all of his opponent's units wins.

This project was made by Keri Warr, Anthony Zhang, Justin Chan, and Jordan Verasamy at AngelHack 2015 Toronto.
